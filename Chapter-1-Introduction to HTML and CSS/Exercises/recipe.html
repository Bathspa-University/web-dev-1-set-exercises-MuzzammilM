Sheer khurma

Sheer khurma is a pakistani sweet dish. It is very special because on any special events or occasions this sweet dish is made in many homes to celebrate. The best thing about this is that it is healthy so you can eat it without any worry of your health. Since many dry fruits are added in this sweet dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 100 grams vermicelli (thin rice noodles)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 10-12 cashew nuts
  • 10-12 almonds, sliced
  • 10-12 pistachios, sliced
  • 10-12 dates, deseeded and chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Method

  1. Heat a large, heavy-bottomed pan or pot over medium heat. Add ghee and let it melt.
  2. Once the ghee is hot, add the vermicelli and roast until it turns golden brown, stirring continuously to avoid burning. This should take about 3-5 minutes. Remove the vermicelli from the pan and set it aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the chopped dates and nuts. Sauté them for a few minutes until they are slightly golden. Remove them from the pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, pour the milk and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.
  5. Add the roasted vermicelli to the milk and cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the vermicelli is soft and cooked through.
  6. Add sugar and cardamom powder to the milk mixture. Stir well to combine and let it simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Finally, add the sautéed dates and nuts to the sheer khurma. Stir well and cook for another minute.
  8. Serve the sheer khurma warm or chilled, garnished with nuts if desired.Enjoy
